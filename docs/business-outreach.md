# VibeVI business outreach package

## Short pitch

VibeVI helps people in the U.S. Virgin Islands decide what to do next—where to eat, sail, stay, shop, unwind, and explore across St. Thomas, St. Croix, St. John, and Water Island. We are building a locally useful directory and planning layer, and we would like to prepare an accurate listing for your business.

## Why VibeVI exists

Visitors, residents, crews, and cruise guests often plan through fragmented search results, social posts, and outdated pages. VibeVI connects island guides and practical ferry/cruise context to source-backed local business profiles. It does not replace the business’s official channels; it routes people to them.

## Free launch listing

- Business name, island, category, and concise factual description
- Area or approved public address
- Official website and approved public contact details
- Business-owned social links
- Placement in relevant island and category discovery
- Internal links from relevant guide/search surfaces where editorially appropriate

Inclusion does not guarantee traffic, ranking, leads, or sales.

## Premium direction—not yet for sale

Future premium profiles may include richer licensed media, expanded story modules, offers, highlighted category placement, or guide sponsorship. Packages, pricing, eligibility, measurement, and paid placement are not launched. Verification cannot be purchased.

## Information requested

- Official business name
- Island and primary category
- 40–120 word factual description
- Area or public address
- Official website
- Public phone/email and explicit permission to republish
- Official Instagram or other business-owned social links
- Coordinates, if known
- Licensed/owned media and required credits
- Best contact for verification
- Written confirmation that the submitted information may be published

## Verification

VibeVI compares submitted information with official/direct sources, records the source and review date, and may ask follow-up questions. Listings remain noindex and direct contact actions stay hidden until the applicable checks pass. Claimed, premium, and verified are separate states.
