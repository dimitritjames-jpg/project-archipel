# VibeVI media sourcing targets

Use this document to source real media without guessing rights or representing businesses inaccurately. Do not download or activate any file until license/permission metadata is recorded in `data/media-manifest.json`.

## Homepage and brand surfaces

| Needed asset | Target file | Recommended source strategy | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Hero water/boat image | `public/media/hero/vibevi-hero-water-boat.jpg` | User-owned photo, commissioned photographer, or licensed stock | Must feel bright, outdoor, turquoise, and USVI-relevant. Do not use a specific charter/business boat unless permission is clear. |
| Ambient sunset/water loop | `public/media/hero/vibevi-hero-sunset-water.mp4` | User-owned video, commissioned video, or licensed stock | Muted background loop only. Avoid identifiable people unless releases are clear. |
| Default Open Graph card | `public/media/og/vibevi-default-1200x630.webp` | Generated art or commissioned brand artwork | Atmosphere is fine; do not imply factual listing content. |

## Guide and utility surfaces

| Surface | Recommended source strategy | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Ferry pages | Generated route/wave art or user-owned dock/ferry photos | Do not imply live vessel tracking. |
| Cruise schedule pages | Harbor/port photo with rights or generated harbor line art | Scheduled capacity only, not actual passenger count. |
| Magens Bay guide | User-owned, commissioned, licensed, or confirmed public-domain/government | Use current/legal source metadata. |
| Buck Island guide | User-owned, commissioned, licensed, or confirmed public-domain/government | Avoid reef/dive claims not supported by source. |
| Virgin Islands National Park guide | Confirmed NPS/public-domain assets if available, otherwise licensed/user-owned | Confirm exact asset license and credit. |

## Business inventory

| Use case | Recommended source strategy | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Verified business cover | Business-provided with written permission or VibeVI-owned shoot | Best path for real listings. |
| Public-info listing image | Avoid until permission is granted | Public-info pages should not look like claimed/partner profiles. |
| Demo listing image | Use abstract fallback art only | Do not create realistic fake business media. |
| Future sponsor creative | Sponsor-provided or commissioned with placement permission | Do not imply sponsor status before launch. |
