# VibeVI outreach workflow

Use this workflow to collect Batch 1 real inventory without making promises VibeVI cannot yet support.

## Who to contact first

Prioritize businesses that:

1. have an official website or active business-owned social account;
2. fit the launch categories: adventure, culinary, culture/nightlife, wellness, stays, local shops;
3. can provide written permission for contact details and media;
4. are likely to benefit from discovery traffic without needing booking/payment tools;
5. help balance St. Thomas, St. Croix, St. John, and Water Island coverage.

## What to ask for

- Official business name.
- Island and best category.
- Short factual description.
- Public address or area.
- Official website and social links.
- Public phone/email and permission to display them.
- Coordinates if known.
- Owned/licensed photos and credit requirements.
- Best contact for verification.
- Written confirmation that submitted information may be published.

## Proof and verification needed

Acceptable sources:

- official website;
- business-owned social account;
- direct written response from the business;
- authoritative registry or official listing.

Do not rely on third-party directories alone. Do not copy promotional claims without source review.

## How to request photos

Ask for:

- 1 cover image, landscape;
- 2-4 optional gallery images;
- confirmation that the business owns or has rights to publish them;
- required photographer credit;
- any usage limits or expiration dates.

Do not use Instagram photos, Google images, or customer photos unless explicit rights are granted.

## How to explain VibeVI

VibeVI is a modern discovery layer for the U.S. Virgin Islands. It helps visitors, locals, crews, and businesses find beaches, boats, bites, nightlife, ferry checks, cruise-day planning, local experiences, and island businesses.

VibeVI is not currently a booking platform, payment platform, owner dashboard, or paid placement marketplace.

## Promise boundaries

Do not promise:

- traffic;
- rankings;
- bookings;
- leads;
- paid placement;
- instant reservations;
- live availability;
- checkout or payments;
- verified status without review.
