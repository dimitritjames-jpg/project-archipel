# VibeVI — Product Constitution

## Product name
**VibeVI** (domain: **MyVibeVI.com**)

## One-sentence identity
Modern U.S. Virgin Islands things-to-do guide and local business discovery engine—beach, boat, bite, night, culture, ferry hops, and island stories.

## Core audience
Visitors, locals, cruise passengers, local businesses, and tourism stakeholders in the USVI.

## Core offering
- Island/category discovery (St. Thomas, St. John, St. Croix, Water Island)
- Business listings with trust states (demo, public-info, verified, claimed, etc.)
- Search, map, experiences pillars, ferry/cruise-day utility pages
- Get-listed intake (not fake booking)

## What this project is NOT
- Not a fake booking platform or live availability engine
- Not a generic travel blog or cheesy tourist brochure
- Not **Project Archipel** (deprecated brand name — repo folder name only)
- Not a dark SaaS radar/command-center product (old direction deprecated)

## Locked tone
Warm Caribbean, refined, local-aware, adventurous, modern—not corny, not hypey.

## Trust / safety rules (hard)
- No fake businesses, reviews, ratings, booking, or live availability
- No fake real-time ferry/cruise claims without source-backed data
- No unlicensed business photos
- Public-info listings disclose public-source status
- Public-info listings do not emit LocalBusiness schema unless strict verification gates allow
- Demo listings: **noindex**, no LocalBusiness schema
- No partner/premium/sponsored claims unless true

## No-drift rules
- Do not invent listings or partnerships for SEO
- Do not add payments/booking checkout without explicit approval
- Do not collapse trust states into “everything looks verified”

## Codex/Cursor may improve freely
- Homepage layout, hero, palette (ocean/turquoise/warm island direction per approved reference)
- Typography, cards, navigation, media placement, section order
- SEO utility pages (ferry, cruise-day) within trust rules
- Performance, accessibility, docs

## Requires explicit approval before changing
- Trust gate logic, schema emission rules, demo/noindex policy
- Claiming “live” transit/cruise data without source ingestion
- Booking, payments, owner dashboard expansion
- Brand rename away from VibeVI / MyVibeVI.com
